Welcome to To-Do List Application!


Stay organized and boost your productivity with our simple and user-friendly To-Do List app. Whether you're managing daily tasks, planning a project, or just keeping track of things to do, this app is designed to help you stay on top of your priorities.

Features:

  • Authentication: Securely sign up and log in to access your personalized task list.
  • Task Management: Easily add, update, and delete tasks as you go.
  • Task Completion: Mark tasks as complete and keep track of what you've accomplished.
  • Responsive Design: Enjoy a seamless experience on both desktop and mobile devices.
  • Personalized Experience: Your tasks are linked to your account, so you can access them from any device after logging in.
